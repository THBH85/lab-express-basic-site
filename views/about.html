Get to know Dražen and why he deserves a memorial

Dražen Petrović was one of the greatest basketball players of all time.

Yet, he is unknown even to most fans of this sport.

Born in Yugoslavia (present-day Croatia), he soon became a star in the european basketball scene.

Naturally, he transfered to the NBA, the strongest league in the world.

Struggling at first, Dražen then managed to compete even against Michael Jordan.

Being only 28 years old, he died in a tragic car accident in Germany.
